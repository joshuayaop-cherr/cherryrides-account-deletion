Cherry Rides respects user privacy and provides an in-app way to delete your account and associated data.

## How to delete your account

Account deletion must be completed **inside the Cherry Rides app** to ensure security and prevent unauthorized requests.

### Steps:
1. Open the Cherry Rides app
2. Log in to your account
3. Go to **Account Settings**
4. Select **Delete Account**
5. Confirm the deletion

Once confirmed, your account and associated data will be permanently deleted.

## Data that is deleted
- User profile information
- Ride requests and ride history
- Messages associated with rides
- Location data associated with rides

## Data retention
Some limited data may be retained temporarily where required for:
- Legal or regulatory compliance
- Fraud prevention
- System backups (automatically removed after a short retention period)
